Cafe

When we conceptualized Vault + Vine, we wanted to create a space where both our guests
and staff would feel a home. Located in the heart of East Falls, our cafe is the perfect place to unwind,
meet a friend, or catch up on some work.

Stop by and enjoy our offerings froma variety of local vendors, including
coffee from Triangle Roasters; tea from The Random Tea Room and pastries
from Au Fournil French Artisan Bakery, and Crust Vegan Bakery.
